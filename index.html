Vision & Philosophy

Scholarship at the Intersection
of Tradition & Modernity

My work seeks to recover the depth and relevance of Indian Knowledge Systems — from Dharmic ethics and environmental philosophy to the living practices of Jainism, Hinduism, and allied traditions — and to bring these into dialogue with contemporary global challenges.
🪔
Dharmic Ethics
Exploring the ethical dimensions of Dharma traditions across Hinduism, Jainism, and Buddhism in a cross-cultural frame.
🌿
Environmental Philosophy
Recovering indigenous ecological wisdom as a resource for contemporary environmental thought and practice.
📚
Global Religions
Comparative study of world religions with a focus on lived traditions, ritual, and community across cultures.
🎓
Academic Leadership
Building institutions and curricula that honour India's intellectual heritage while engaging global conversations.
